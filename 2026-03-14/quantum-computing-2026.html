Quantum Computing News: ICQE 2025 & Latest Quantum Research

2025.06.17 · Blog quantum computing news quantum research

在硬件突破方面，Google的105量子比特Willow芯片展示了指数级错误率降低，在大约5分钟内完成了一项经典超级计算机需要约10的25次方年才能完成的基准测试。这一里程碑被称为「低于阈值」——量子纠错的错误率首次低于维持量子运算所需的水平，证明了容错量子计算在原则上是可行的。IBM发布了其容错量子路线图「Starling」，目标是在2029年构建200个逻辑量子比特的系统，支持1亿次操作。微软则推出了Majorana 1，一种基于马约拉纳费米子的拓扑量子比特架构，理论上具有内在的容错能力，目标是扩展到数百万量子比特。

Quantum computing is rapidly advancing on multiple fronts, from hardware innovations to real-world demonstrations and government strategies. In June 2025, the International Conference on Quantum Energy (ICQE 2025) in Padua, Italy, drew global attention to the need for quantum research to align with energy and sustainability goals.

In this article, we cover key takeaways from ICQE 2025 and a broader roundup of recent quantum computing news and quantum research.

ICQE 2025 &ndash; Energy & Responsibility: The conference emphasized that "the cost of energy will define the cost of intelligence," urging quantum development with sustainability in mind. Researchers discussed using quantum tools for renewable energy challenges and making computing more efficient.

在实际应用方面，量子计算正在几个领域展示超越经典方法的潜力。2025年3月，IonQ和Ansys在IonQ的36量子比特计算机上运行了医疗设备模拟，实现了比经典高性能计算约12%的加速。虽然加速幅度不大，但这是首批展示量子计算在实际工程场景中超越经典方法的案例之一。密歇根大学的研究人员利用量子模拟解决了关于准晶体的40年难题，通过尖端量子算法模拟其原子结构。在药物发现领域，量子模拟正在帮助科学家更精确地计算分子的电子结构，为设计新药物提供了新的计算工具。

Major Tech Breakthroughs: Google's 105-qubit Willow chip demonstrated exponential error reduction and ran a benchmark in ~5 minutes that would take a classical supercomputer ~10^25 years. IBM unveiled its fault-tolerant roadmap Starling , a 2029 target system with 200 logical qubits (100 million operations). Microsoft introduced Majorana 1 , a new topological qubit architecture aiming to scale to millions of qubits.

Quantum Advantage in Action: In March 2025, IonQ and Ansys ran a medical-device simulation on IonQ's 36-qubit computer, achieving a ~12% speed-up over classical HPC. This is one of the first cases showing a practical quantum outperforming a classical method. Researchers also used quantum simulation to solve a 40-year puzzle about quasicrystals: University of Michigan scientists proved these exotic materials are fundamentally stable by simulating their atomic structure with cutting-edge quantum algorithms.

在硬件里程碑方面，富士通和RIKEN宣布了256量子比特的超导量子计算机，是2023年系统的四倍，并计划在2026年构建1000量子比特的机器。IBM的量子处理器已经达到了1121量子比特的规模。这种规模的扩展使得在混合量子-经典平台上处理更复杂的问题成为可能。但量子比特数量本身不是唯一重要的指标——量子门的保真度、相干时间和连接拓扑同样关键。一个拥有1000个低质量量子比特的系统可能不如一个拥有100个高质量量子比特的系统实用。

Hardware Milestones: In April 2025, Fujitsu and RIKEN announced a 256-qubit superconducting quantum computer &ndash; four times larger than their 2023 &ndash; and set plans for a 1,000-qubit machine by 2026. These scale-ups enable tackling more complex problems (larger molecules, advanced error correction) on hybrid quantum-classical platforms.

ICQE 2025: Energy Meets Quantum Responsibility

能源效率是量子计算发展中一个日益受关注的问题。ICQE 2025国际量子能源会议的核心信息是：随着量子计算和AI规模的扩大，它们的能源需求不容忽视。会议联合主席Francesco Campaioli直言：「最终定义智能成本的是能源成本。」偏置猫量子比特纠错等架构被证明可以降低逻辑量子比特的能耗开销。量子模拟在新材料发现方面的应用也为能源效率提升提供了新途径。在政府战略层面，美国、欧盟和中国都在加大对量子计算的投资，将其视为国家竞争力的关键要素。量子计算正在从科学幻想变为工程现实，我们正在见证计算技术的一次根本性变革。

ICQE 2025 drove home that as quantum and AI scale up, their energy demands cannot be overlooked. Co-chair Francesco Campaioli framed it starkly: "It is the cost of energy that ultimately will define the cost of intelligence". Talks stressed that quantum technology should be built from the ground up for efficiency and sustainability, not retrofitted later.

For example, speakers highlighted three key areas: foundational physics, efficient computing, and materials discovery. Understanding quantum thermodynamics could lead to new low-energy cooling and power distribution strategies.

在硬件里程碑方面，富士通和RIKEN宣布了256量子比特的超导量子计算机，是2023年系统的四倍，并计划在2026年构建1000量子比特的机器。IBM的量子处理器已经达到了1121量子比特的规模。但量子比特数量本身不是唯一重要的指标——量子门的保真度、相干时间和连接拓扑同样关键。一个拥有1000个低质量量子比特的系统可能不如一个拥有100个高质量量子比特的系统实用。

Architectures like biased cat-qubit error correction were shown to reduce the energy overhead of logical qubits. And quantum simulation/sensing offers faster ways to design next-generation energy materials &ndash; from solar cells to hydrogen catalysts &ndash; by modeling their quantum behavior directly.

ICQE also underscored the need for collaboration across fields. Scientists, engineers, industry and policymakers were urged to break out of silos and share a unified vision. On the political side, Italy used the event to announce an upcoming National Quantum Strategy. Physicist Simone Montangero revealed that Italy will create an "Italian Quantum Alliance" uniting universities, research institutes and companies under this strategy. This move &ndash; integrating quantum into broader technology and energy policies &ndash; reflects a trend. As one insider put it, "signals like this influence how partnerships are formed and how seriously the public and private sectors take the work ahead".

In short, ICQE 2025 highlighted that quantum's future is not just faster computing, but smarter, sustainable growth aligned with real-world needs.

Latest Breakthroughs in Quantum Hardware and Algorithms

Top quantum computing companies reported major quantum leaps. In December 2024, Google announced Willow , a 105-qubit superconducting processor, as its new flagship. Willow's standout achievement is exponential error correction: the more physical qubits used, the lower the overall error rate &ndash; a milestone known as going "below threshold".

在软件和算法方面，量子错误纠正码的实用化取得了重大进展。表面码和拓扑码的实现使得逻辑量子比特的错误率降到了实用阈值以下。量子编程框架如Qiskit、Cirq和PennyLane也在快速成熟，使得更多开发者能够编写和优化量子算法。量子-经典混合算法，特别是变分量子本征求解器（VQE）和量子近似优化算法（QAOA），正在成为近期量子计算应用的主流方案——它们能够在噪声中等规模量子（NISQ）设备上提供有价值的计算结果。

Google also ran the standard random-circuit sampling benchmark: Willow solved it in under 5 minutes, a task that the fastest classical supercomputer would take ~10^25 years to match. These results, described in Nature , are strong evidence that large, error-corrected quantum computers can be built in the future.
