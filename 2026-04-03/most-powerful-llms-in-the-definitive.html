中文原文

2026年，大语言模型（LLM）领域的最大叙事已不再是"哪个模型最强"，而是"闭源与开源之间的差距是否已经闭合"。答案正在变得清晰：在许多基准测试上，两者已旗鼓相当，而在成本和可定制性上，开源模型正建立起不可忽视的优势。

Anthropic在2025年8月发布了Claude 4系列（Opus与Sonnet），以200K超长上下文窗口和卓越的编程能力著称。2026年3月推出的Claude Opus 4.1进一步提升了复杂推理任务的稳定性，巩固了其作为企业级首选编程模型的市场地位。Claude系列的核心竞争力在于"可预测性"——对复杂指令的遵循能力和输出稳定性，使其在法律、金融等高风险场景中备受青睐。

OpenAI的GPT-5家族贯穿整个2025年，并在2026年3月推出GPT-5.4，将通用模型与编程模型整合为单一旗舰，首次引入原生计算机使用能力。GPT-5系列的标志性特征是可配置的推理努力和跨越百万Token的上下文窗口，使其在多步骤代理工作流程中表现出色。

Google DeepMind的Gemini 3.1 Pro于2026年2月发布，在18项跟踪基准中的12项取得领先，并将ARC-AGI-2性能提升超过一倍。Gemini 3 Flash则以"Pro级智能、Flash级速度和成本"成为Google消费产品的默认模型，展示了效率与性能的精妙平衡。

开源阵营同样精彩。Meta的Llama 4全面转向混合专家（MoE）架构，原生多模态，并提供最高1000万Token的上下文窗口。Mistral AI在2025年12月推出的Mistral 3系列则以10个模型的完整阵容刷新了开源标杆，全部采用Apache 2.0许可证，其中Devstral 2专攻编程任务，在代码生成领域展现出强劲竞争力。

DeepSeek V3.2（以MIT许可证发布）在推理成本上持续领跑，而阿里巴巴的Qwen3-235B-A22B则以与o1对标的推理性能，在开源社区赢得了极高声誉。Nvidia的Nemotron 3系列则提供了包含权重、训练数据集和强化学习环境的完整开源包，以Mamba-Transformer混合MoE架构，专门针对Agent工作负载进行了优化。

对于企业的关键启示是：模型选择已不能仅看原始性能排名。上下文需求、推理成本、许可合规性和基础设施匹配度，正在成为决策链条中同等重要的变量。

English Original

The defining LLM narrative of 2026 is not which model ranks first — it is whether the gap between frontier closed-source and open-source models has finally closed. The answer is becoming clear: on many benchmarks, parity is near; on cost and customizability, open models are building decisive advantages.

Anthropic's Claude 4 family — Opus and Sonnet — launched August 2025 with a 200K-token context window and best-in-class coding performance. Claude Opus 4.1 (March 2026) further refined complex reasoning stability, cementing Claude as the enterprise-preferred coding model. Claude's edge lies in predictability — instruction-following reliability and output consistency make it the choice for high-stakes legal and financial deployments.

OpenAI's GPT-5 family spanned 2025 and converged in GPT-5.4 (March 2026), unifying general-purpose and coding models into a single flagship with native computer use. Configurable reasoning effort and a million-token context window make GPT-5 the backbone of complex multi-step agentic workflows.

Google DeepMind's Gemini 3.1 Pro (February 2026) leads on 12 of 18 tracked benchmarks, doubling ARC-AGI-2 performance over its predecessor. Gemini 3 Flash delivers Pro-level intelligence at Flash speed and cost, serving as the default model across Google's consumer products.

In open source, Meta's Llama 4 made a decisive MoE architectural leap with native multimodal training and a 10-million-token context window. Mistral AI's Mistral 3 family (December 2025) released 10 Apache 2.0-licensed models, with Devstral 2 standing out in coding. DeepSeek V3.2 continues to dominate on inference cost, while Alibaba's Qwen3-235B-A22B matches o1 on reasoning benchmarks. Nvidia's Nemotron 3 rounds out the field with a full open-source package — weights, training data, and RL environments — optimized for agent workloads via a Mamba-Transformer hybrid MoE architecture.

The enterprise takeaway: model selection in 2026 requires weighing not just benchmark performance, but context window needs, inference cost, licensing compliance, and infrastructure fit — variables that are now equally decisive.
