2026年人工智能代理的采用：数据显示了什么

AI Agent Adoption in 2026: What the Data Shows

2026年2月20日| Julian Johari
来自Gartner、Forrester、IDC、Deloitte和技术领导者的研究和预测

February 20, 2026 | Julian Johari
Research and predictions from Gartner, Forrester, IDC, Deloitte, and technology leaders

人工智能代理正在走出实验室，进入业务运营。 不是您习惯的聊天机器人，这些系统可以规划一系列任务，根据不断变化的条件做出决策，并在没有持续监督的情况下执行工作。
他们正在处理从发票核对到安全监控的所有工作，而且这种转变的发生速度比大多数组织预期的要快。主要研究公司（ Gartner、Forrester、IDC、Deloitte ）与企业技术领导者一致认为： 2026年是代理商从实验项目大规模转向生产部署的一年。但有一个问题。
如果公司不能正确掌握治理和投资回报的基本面，到2027年，这些举措中有40%以上可能会被放弃。 专家客服代表正在学习如何共同努力

AI agents are moving out of the lab and into business operations. Not the chatbots you are used to, these systems can plan a sequence of tasks, make decisions based on changing conditions, and execute work without continuous oversight.
They are handling everything from invoice reconciliation to security monitoring, and this shift is happening faster than most organizations expected. Major research firms (Gartner, Forrester, IDC, Deloitte) and enterprise technology leaders agree: 2026 is the year agents make the large-scale transition from experimental projects to production deployments. But there is a catch.
If companies fail to get the fundamentals of governance and ROI right, over 40% of these initiatives could be abandoned by 2027. Specialist agents are learning how to work together

单一用途代理模型已过时。 Forrester和Gartner都认为2026年是多代理系统的突破之年，专业代理在中央协调下进行协作。
一位客服代表负责确认潜在客户资格，另一位客服代表负责起草个性化外联活动，第三位客服代表负责确认合规要求。他们保持共享的背景，并在没有人为干预的情况下交接工作。AWS和IBM的领导者指出，编排层是这里的关键基础设施，类似于Kubernetes为容器管理所做的工作。
治理将决定哪些项目能够生存

The single-purpose agent model is obsolete. Both Forrester and Gartner identify 2026 as the breakout year for multi-agent systems, where specialized agents collaborate under central coordination.
One agent qualifies leads, another drafts personalized outreach campaigns, and a third verifies compliance requirements. They maintain shared context and hand off work without human intervention. Leaders at AWS and IBM point to the orchestration layer as the key infrastructure here, analogous to what Kubernetes did for container management.
Governance will determine which projects survive

以下是Gartner分析中令人不安的事实：到2027年，超过40%的代理项目将失败。 原因可以预测：成本失控、业务价值不明确，以及客服代表的行为方式违反政策或造成风险。 由于代理具有一定程度的自主性，因此出现问题的可能性是不变的。
基础知识包括实时监控系统、可以立即停止代理操作的终止开关以及全面的审计跟踪。 经济需求注意力

Here is the unsettling truth from Gartner's analysis: by 2027, over 40% of agent projects will fail. The reasons are predictable: runaway costs, unclear business value, and agents behaving in ways that violate policies or create risk. Because agents operate with a degree of autonomy, the potential for things to go wrong is ever-present.
The basics include real-time monitoring systems, kill switches that can immediately halt agent operations, and comprehensive audit trails. Economics demand attention

代理持续运行，生成API调用，消耗计算代币，全天候累积云基础架构成本。 IDC预测，到2027年，代理使用量将增长10倍，推理需求将增长1000倍。
获得这种权利的组织正在实施分层策略：低成本模型处理日常任务，而高级模型则保留用于高风险决策。 从回报最清晰的地方开始

Agents run continuously, generating API calls, consuming compute tokens, and accumulating cloud infrastructure costs around the clock. IDC predicts that by 2027, agent usage will grow 10x and inference demand will grow 1000x.
Organizations getting this right are implementing tiered strategies: low-cost models handle routine tasks, while premium models are reserved for high-stakes decisions. Start where returns are clearest

试点阶段已经结束。
分析师正在确定客服代表在2026年交付可衡量结果的具体用例：处理退款和升级的客服代表每月为小团队节省40多个小时。财务和运营自动化发票和费用审计将结算流程加快了30-50%。安全和治理异常检测可实现主动降低风险。
销售和营销线索生成和认证系统可使管道速度提高2-3倍。 接下来是物理操作

The pilot phase is over.
Analysts are identifying specific use cases where agents deliver measurable results in 2026: customer service agents handling refunds and escalations save small teams 40+ hours per month. Finance and operations agents automating invoice and expense audits accelerate settlement processes by 30-50%. Security and governance agents detect anomalies for proactive risk mitigation.
Sales and marketing agents for lead generation and qualification increase pipeline velocity by 2-3x. Next comes physical operations

Forrester强调“物理AI”是一个值得关注的领域：实时协调机器人、传感器和供应链系统的代理商。
在德勤的企业AI现状调查中，超过一半（ 58% ）的受访者表示，他们的公司已经在某种程度上使用了物理AI ，预计两年内采用率将达到80%。 您的员工需要新技能

Forrester highlights "physical AI" as a space to watch: agents coordinating robots, sensors, and supply chain systems in real time.
In Deloitte's State of AI in the Enterprise survey, more than half (58%) of respondents said their companies are already using physical AI to some extent, with adoption expected to reach 80% within two years. Your workforce needs new skills

代理增强而不是取代人类的工作。
Gartner和Forrester都强调，员工需要接受如何设计代理工作流程、监督其运营以及使用自动化系统进行有效协作的培训。新的角色正在出现：代理架构师、性能工程师和监督专家。但是，您不需要机器学习学位或开发人员团队来开始构建代理。
获得最快回报的组织将座席创建工具直接交到最了解问题的业务用户手中。 这对您的组织意味着什么

Agents augment rather than replace human work.
Both Gartner and Forrester emphasize that employees need training in how to design agent workflows, oversee their operations, and collaborate effectively with automated systems. New roles are emerging: agent architects, performance engineers, and oversight specialists. But you do not need a machine learning degree or a team of developers to start building agents.
The organizations achieving the fastest returns put agent creation tools directly in the hands of business users who understand the problems best. What this means for your organization

研究一致： 2026年是代理商从实验技术过渡到运营基础设施的一年。 但应该认真对待Gartner关于项目失败率的警告。
前进的道路是明确的。将重点放在有投资回报记录的领域的受管试点上。在扩展之前获取您的数据基础架构。衡量一切，并愿意关闭无效的东西。成功来自于将客服代表视为责任明确的系统，而不是定义不清的问题的解决方案。

The research is consistent: 2026 is the year agents transition from experimental technology to operational infrastructure. But Gartner's warning about project failure rates should be taken seriously.
The path forward is clear. Focus on managed pilots in areas with a proven track record of ROI. Get your data infrastructure in order before scaling. Measure everything, and be willing to shut down what does not work. Success comes from treating agents as systems with clear accountability, not as solutions to ill-defined problems.
