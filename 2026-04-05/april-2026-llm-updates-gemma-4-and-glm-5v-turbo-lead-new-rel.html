2026年4月开源AI模型更新：Gemma 4系列、GLM-5V-Turbo及最新榜单

2026年4月的LLM更新带来了多款重要的开源模型发布，其中Google的Gemma 4系列和智谱AI的GLM-5V-Turbo最受瞩目。这些新模型在不同维度上推动了开源AI的能力边界，为开发者提供了更丰富的选择。

April 2026 LLM Updates: Gemma 4 and GLM-5V-Turbo Lead New Releases

April 2, 2026 – Five releases in a single day marked the most significant open-source AI advancement of 2026 so far.

Gemma 4系列包括从2B到27B参数的多个模型变体，覆盖了从边缘设备到云端部署的全场景需求。Gemma 4 27B在多项基准测试中的表现已经超越了上一代的Gemma 2 27B，特别是在数学推理和代码生成方面的提升尤为显著。这种提升主要归功于改进的训练数据配比和更高效的MoE（混合专家）架构——虽然总参数量为27B，但在推理时仅激活其中的一部分，实现了性能和效率的良好平衡。Gemma 4 2B虽然参数量小，但在同类规模的模型中表现突出，特别适合在移动设备和IoT场景中部署。Google还为Gemma 4系列提供了更友好的商业许可，降低了企业使用的门槛。

Google Gemma 4 Series Launch
On April 2, 2026, Google released four variants of Gemma 4, its latest open-source language model family:

Gemma 4 26B-A4B: The standout performer with a GPQA score of 0.8, representing the state of the art in scientific reasoning for open models under 30 billion parameters. The "A4B" designation indicates an innovative architecture optimized for multi-task performance.

Gemma 4 31B: A balanced large model variant delivering strong performance across benchmarks with a GPQA score of 0.8.

GLM-5V-Turbo是智谱AI推出的多模态模型，在中文场景下的视觉理解能力表现尤为突出。它能够精确识别和理解中文文档、截图和图表中的内容，在OCR、文档分析和图表解读等任务上达到了业界领先水平。对于需要处理大量中文商业文档的企业来说，GLM-5V-Turbo是一个理想的选择。它的推理效率也经过特别优化，支持高吞吐量的生产环境部署，在不牺牲精度的前提下实现了快速响应。

Gemma 4 E2B (Efficiency-2B): A compact 2-billion parameter model with impressive efficiency (GPQA: 0.4), designed for edge deployment and mobile applications where compute resources are limited.

Gemma 4 E4B (Efficiency-4B): A 4-billion parameter variant offering a middle ground between the ultra-compact E2B and the larger models, with GPQA: 0.6.

在开源LLM排行榜上，Llama 4系列仍然保持着综合能力的前列位置，特别是在英文场景下的表现。Meta持续对Llama系列进行改进，包括更好的指令遵循能力、更长的上下文窗口和更精细的安全过滤。DeepSeek-V4在数学和代码推理方面保持领先，其创新的MoE架构使它在特定任务上的性价比极高。Qwen 3系列在多语言支持（尤其是中文）方面持续优化，特别是Qwen3-VL在视觉理解方面的表现令人印象深刻。

Gemma 4 represents Google's continued commitment to open-weight model development, building on the success of previous Gemma generations with significant improvements in reasoning, efficiency, and multi-modal capabilities.

Zhipu AI GLM-5V-Turbo
Zhipu AI (智谱AI), China's leading LLM developer, released GLM-5V-Turbo on the same day. This proprietary model focuses on vision-language capabilities with fast inference, targeting enterprise applications requiring rapid multi-modal processing.

这些开源模型的持续进步正在改变AI应用的开发模式。越来越多的企业选择基于开源模型进行定制化微调，而非直接依赖闭源API。这种转变不仅降低了成本，还提供了更大的灵活性——企业可以根据自己的需求调整模型的行为，而不受闭源API提供商的限制。数据隐私是另一个重要因素——使用开源模型意味着敏感数据不需要发送到第三方服务器。开源AI生态的蓬勃发展，是2026年AI领域最令人鼓舞的趋势之一——它正在使高质量的AI能力变得人人可用。

Context: Why April 2026 Matters
The concentration of four major open-source releases from Google in a single day signals intensifying competition in the open-weight model space. With Gemma 4 joining previously released models like Qwen3-Max-Thinking, Llama 4, and DeepSeek-R2, April 2026 has become a pivotal month for open-source AI.

The GPQA (General Purpose Question Answering) benchmark has emerged as a key measure of model capability, testing not just factual recall but reasoning and problem-solving across domains. Gemma 4 26B-A4B's score of 0.8 places it among the top open models globally, challenging proprietary systems on core cognitive tasks.
