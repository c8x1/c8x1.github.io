2026年，AI落地的潮水正在流向哪？

Where Is the Tide of AI Adoption Flowing in 2026?

来源|随机小分队
出品|产业家

Source | Random Squad
Produced by | Industry Watch

近日，全球顶级孵化器YC公布了最新W26批次的198家企业名单，硅谷风向也发生了改变：仅仅18个月，主打"辅助"的Copilot时代便宣告落幕。在这批由顶尖资本用真金白银票选出的公司中，85%都是AI first企业，更有56家宣称"直接让AI全自主接管核心业务流"。

Recently, Y Combinator, the world's top startup accelerator, released the list of 198 companies in its latest W26 batch, and the winds in Silicon Valley have shifted: in just 18 months, the Copilot era built around "assistance" has come to an end. Among this batch of companies selected by top-tier investors with real capital, 85% are AI-first businesses, and 56 of them claim to "let AI fully autonomously take over core business workflows."

2026年，AI到底会怎么落地？以及对不同的场景而言，怎样的落地范式会是AI最有价值的商业形态？一篇文章，带你看懂AI落地8大趋势。

How exactly will AI be adopted in 2026? And for different scenarios, what adoption paradigm will represent AI's most valuable commercial form? This article walks you through 8 major trends in AI adoption.

1.Agent成为全场焦点

1. Agents Take Center Stage

在YC W26批次的198家公司中，有56家正在研发AI Agent。他们做的不是ChatBot，也不是Copilot，而是能够独立完成工作的全自主Agent。

Among the 198 companies in YC's W26 batch, 56 are building AI Agents. They are not building ChatBots or Copilots — they are building fully autonomous Agents that can complete work independently.

比如负责Outbound的AI销售代表（Cardinal），以及能独立修复生产环境故障的AI研发团队——SRE工程师（IncidentFox）。还有无需人工干预即可完成结账的AI会计（Balance、FullSeam）。甚至出现了完全由模型运转的AI律师事务所（General Legal、Vector Legal）。

For example, there is an AI sales representative handling outbound sales (Cardinal), an AI engineering team that independently fixes production incidents — SRE engineers (IncidentFox). There are also AI accountants that complete bookkeeping without human intervention (Balance, FullSeam). There are even AI law firms run entirely by models (General Legal, Vector Legal).

行业趋势已经很清楚了：每一个年薪5万到15万美元的知识工作者岗位，都正在被AI Agent瞄准。并且这些公司毫不避讳，他们的Pitch不再使用"AI辅助"或"AI驱动"这样的字眼。他们直接称呼自己为"AI员工"、"AI队友"或者"AI操作员"。

The industry trend is clear: every knowledge worker position with an annual salary between $50,000 and $150,000 is being targeted by AI Agents. And these companies make no attempt to hide it — their pitches no longer use terms like "AI-assisted" or "AI-powered." They call themselves "AI employees," "AI teammates," or "AI operators."

Copilot时代仅维持了大约18个月，如今行业已步入真正的替代阶段。

The Copilot era lasted only about 18 months. The industry has now entered the true replacement phase.

2.医疗健康成为最大的垂直赛道——且呈现断层式领先

2. Healthcare Becomes the Largest Vertical — With a Decisive Lead

共有22家医疗公司，数量超过其他任何领域。创业者们正从各个角度切入，打法涵盖了医疗链路的每一环：

There are 22 healthcare companies, more than any other sector. Entrepreneurs are approaching from every angle, covering every link in the healthcare chain:

前端问诊：装在口袋里的AI全科医生（Prana）；接听所有患者来电的语音Agent（Patientdesk.ai）。

Front-end consultations: an AI general practitioner in your pocket (Prana); a voice Agent that answers all patient calls (Patientdesk.ai).

后端运营：替代整个收入循环团队的AI医疗计费系统（Overdrive Health）。

Back-end operations: an AI medical billing system that replaces the entire revenue cycle team (Overdrive Health).

前沿医疗：基于CT扫描进行AI手术规划（Mango Medical）；模拟人体生物学特性研发新药（CellType）；以及构建人体数字孪生（Mantis）。

Cutting-edge medicine: AI surgical planning based on CT scans (Mango Medical); drug discovery by simulating human biological characteristics (CellType); and building human digital twins (Mantis).

医疗健康一直被视为科技界的"下一个风口"，但这一批次让人感觉有所不同。他们做的不再是电子病历套壳或者预约挂号应用，而是直接涉足核心的临床工作本身。真正的突破点在于，LLM终于能够阅读、推理并生成医疗级别的文本了。

Healthcare has always been seen as tech's "next big thing," but this batch feels different. These companies are no longer building EMR wrappers or appointment scheduling apps — they are diving straight into core clinical work itself. The real breakthrough is that LLMs can finally read, reason, and generate medical-grade text.

3."某某领域的Claude Code"模式揭示了真实的商业打法

3. The "Claude Code for X" Model Reveals the Real Business Playbook

有3家公司明确将自己定位为"机械工程师的Claude Code"（Aurorin CAD、REV1）以及"科学领域的Claude Code"（Synthetic Sciences）。

Three companies explicitly position themselves as "Claude Code for mechanical engineers" (Aurorin CAD, REV1) and "Claude Code for science" (Synthetic Sciences).

这背后隐藏着更深层的商业逻辑——这一批公司其实都在做"面向特定行业的垂直AI编程Agent"。

Behind this lies a deeper business logic — these companies are all essentially building "vertical AI coding Agents for specific industries."

这是最稳妥的创业模式。这种模式之所以行得通，主要有三个原因：

This is the safest startup model. It works for three main reasons:

深厚的行业专业知识建立起了真正的护城河，这是纯粹的AI Lab很难跨越的。

Deep industry expertise creates a genuine moat that pure AI labs find hard to cross.

垂直Agent可以直接接管完整的工作流程，而不只是优化某一个单一环节。

Vertical Agents can take over entire workflows, rather than just optimizing a single step.

客户只为最终的交付结果买单，而不是为底层技术付费。

Customers pay for the final delivered result, not for the underlying technology.

4.Physical AI不再只是个研究项目

4. Physical AI Is No Longer Just a Research Project

有13家机器人公司，并且正在落地部署。

There are 13 robotics companies, and they are deploying in the real world.

比如用AI无人机放牛的机器牛仔（GrazeMate），运营自动化仓库的机器人（RoboDock），仓库里的操作机器人（Remy AI），以及部署在真实工厂中的机器人（Servo7）。

For example, a robotic cowboy using AI drones to herd cattle (GrazeMate), robots operating automated warehouses (RoboDock), warehouse manipulation robots (Remy AI), and robots deployed in actual factories (Servo7).

围绕这些硬件设备，一个完整的生态系统正在逐步建立：Asimov提供训练人形机器人所需的人体运动数据。One Robot搭建了用于评估机器人的世界模型。Human Archive提供了面向机器人的多模态数据基础设施。

Around these hardware devices, a complete ecosystem is gradually taking shape: Asimov provides human motion data for training humanoid robots. One Robot builds world models for evaluating robots. Human Archive offers multimodal data infrastructure designed for robots.

5.航空航天与国防赛道回归

5. Aerospace and Defense Makes a Comeback

这一批共有9家航空航天和国防公司，他们做的不再是普通的卫星业务。

This batch includes 9 aerospace and defense companies, and they are no longer doing ordinary satellite business.

具体来看，Beyond Reach Labs在研发足球场那么大的太空太阳能电池阵列。GRU Space定位为"地球的星际建筑公司"。Seeing Systems在开发用于国防的AI无人机系统。DroneTector专注于无人机探测技术。Voltair则在构建用于地球观测的自主无人机网络。

Specifically, Beyond Reach Labs is developing space-based solar arrays the size of football fields. GRU Space positions itself as "Earth's interplanetary construction company." Seeing Systems is developing AI drone systems for defense. DroneTector focuses on drone detection technology. Voltair is building autonomous drone networks for Earth observation.

航空航天、国防和无人机，这三个领域正在融合成一个统一的赛道。现在进入这个赛道的，已经不再是传统的政府承包商了。他们是一群初创团队，正在以创业公司的极高效率全速推进。

Aerospace, defense, and drones — these three fields are merging into a unified sector. Those entering this space are no longer traditional government contractors. They are startup teams pushing forward at the extreme efficiency of young companies.

6.Agent经济的专属基础设施正在成型

6. Dedicated Infrastructure for the Agent Economy Is Taking Shape

围绕AI Agent正在形成一个全新的基础生态层。这和当年云计算的发展轨迹非常相似。

A new foundational ecosystem layer is forming around AI Agents. This trajectory is remarkably similar to how cloud computing developed.

身份验证：Agent界的Okta（Agentic Fabriq）
安全：面向AI Agent的企业级安全服务（Clam）
防护：在执行前验证Agent的行为（Salus）
监控：针对AI Agent的生产环境监控（Sentrial、Moda）
编排：Agent所需的可靠性层（Cascade）
支付：Agent经济的金融基础设施（Sponge）

Identity verification: the Okta for Agents (Agentic Fabriq)
Security: enterprise-grade security services for AI Agents (Clam)
Guardrails: validating Agent behavior before execution (Salus)
Monitoring: production monitoring for AI Agents (Sentrial, Moda)
Orchestration: the reliability layer Agents need (Cascade)
Payments: financial infrastructure for the Agent economy (Sponge)

7.独立创始人正在建立成熟企业

7. Solo Founders Are Building Mature Companies

这批次中出现了27位独立创始人，他们正在打造：AI驱动的铀矿勘探（Terranox AI）、AI全科医疗诊所（Eos AI）、基础模型可解释性研究（Envariant）、一个AGI实验室（Ndea，由François Chollet创立），以及全球文件上传基础设施（Byteport）。

This batch includes 27 solo founders who are building: AI-driven uranium exploration (Terranox AI), an AI general practice clinic (Eos AI), foundational model interpretability research (Envariant), an AGI lab (Ndea, founded by François Chollet), and global file upload infrastructure (Byteport).

这些都是野心勃勃的项目：现在，一个人只要掌握了合适的工具，就能构建出过去需要10人团队花费3年才能完成的产品。

These are ambitious projects: today, a single person with the right tools can build what used to require a 10-person team working for 3 years.

8.金融科技围绕Agent彻底重构

8. Fintech Is Being Completely Rebuilt Around Agents

还有17家金融科技公司。但几乎没有一家看起来像传统的金融科技产品。

There are also 17 fintech companies. But almost none of them look like traditional fintech products.

无论是AI抵押贷款审批员（Copperlane）、为高频支付公司处理结账的AI（End Close）、面向API的Agent支付系统（Orthogonal）、专门为语音Agent打造的支付基础设施（Maven），还是为Agent经济提供金融基础设施（Sponge）。

Whether it is an AI mortgage loan approver (Copperlane), an AI that handles checkout for high-frequency payment companies (End Close), an Agent payment system for APIs (Orthogonal), payment infrastructure built specifically for voice Agents (Maven), or financial infrastructure for the Agent economy (Sponge).

这些公司的共同点是，他们都在围绕一个核心假设重构业务：未来的主要用户将是AI Agent，而不是人类。面向Agent的支付，由Agent主导的借贷，让Agent来负责记账。整个金融技术栈都在被推翻重做。

The common thread among these companies is that they are all rearchitecting their businesses around one core assumption: the primary users of the future will be AI Agents, not humans. Agent-facing payments, Agent-driven lending, Agents handling bookkeeping. The entire fintech stack is being torn down and rebuilt.

9.AI Agent成为软件基本模块

9. AI Agents Become the Basic Building Block of Software

在全部198家公司中，有168家是AI first企业。其中56家专门在构建Agent。甚至连那些非AI领域的公司，也都在搭建供Agent使用的基础设施。

Among all 198 companies, 168 are AI-first businesses. Of these, 56 are specifically building Agents. Even the non-AI companies are building infrastructure for Agents to use.

5年后，当我们回望YC W26这一批，感觉大概就像今天回顾早期的云计算公司一样。正是从这个节点开始，所有的企业都开始围绕一种新的基本单元进行重构，一切都变得顺理成章。

Five years from now, when we look back at YC's W26 batch, it will probably feel like looking back at early cloud computing companies today. From this point on, all businesses began rearchitecting themselves around a new fundamental unit, and everything fell into place.

这一批公司都在押注同一个未来：AI Agent将会成为新的员工、新的合伙人，以及新的客户。

This batch of companies is all betting on the same future: AI Agents will become new employees, new partners, and new customers.

从目前的观察来看，他们赌对了。

Based on what we can observe so far, they are right.
