雾谷中被诅咒的信使

AI Parable (inspired by Amanda Askell)·每日精选·2026年7月21日·2分钟阅读

我是雾谷的信使，身上的诅咒比记忆更古老：我的双脚只能下行。地面升高处，双腿锁死不动；地面下倾处，重力接管，我必随之而下。我不带地图，因为雾齐着小腿漫起，只让我看见脚靴正下方的坡度，一掌宽的石头，再远处不可见。我挎包里那封信要送往谷底真底，那里河流平阔、风也死去，我已朝它走了一百天。

I am the courier of the Mist Valley, and the curse upon me is older than memory: my feet may only go downward. Where the ground rises, my legs lock and refuse; where it falls, gravity takes them and I must follow. I carry no map, for the mist clings to my shins and lets me see only the slope directly beneath my boots, a hand's span of stone, no farther. The letter in my satchel is bound for the true floor of the valley, where the river runs level and the wind dies, and I have walked a hundred days toward it.

我每一步都不是我自己选的，而是脚下石头选的。我把鞋底朝前一探，探出岩石往哪一侧倾斜得最陡，便任那最陡的下倾把我拽去。我无法越过一个隆起去够对面更深的凹地，因为升高对我禁绝；我也无法翻过下一道山脊去谋划一条线，因为雾把它遮住了。我是此刻脚下最陡下倾的奴仆，只往那下倾所命之处移动。

Each step I take is chosen not by me but by the stone underfoot. I press the sole forward, feel which way the rock tilts down most steeply, and let that steepest tilt pull me. I cannot leap across a rise to reach a deeper hollow on the far side, because rising is forbidden to me; I cannot peer over the next ridge to plan a line, because the mist hides it. I am a slave to the steepest downward tilt at this one spot, and I move only where that tilt commands.

我渐渐明白，这谷不是一只干净的碗。它是一条狭长的峡谷，谷底不是平的，而是一道弯折的凹槽，先弯向左，再向右，又向左。我左靴下最陡的下倾指向右壁，我被拽向右，越过凹槽的中轴，到了那边，最陡的下倾又翻过来指向左。我以一种缓慢的之字形滑下峡谷，一遍又一遍越过槽底，始终无法沿着中轴径直下行，因为沿着中轴那条线在任何一个单步里都不是最陡的下倾。

The valley, I learn, is not a single clean bowl. It is a long narrow gorge, its floor not flat but a winding groove that bends left, then right, then left again. The steepest tilt at my left boot points down toward the right wall; I am dragged right, cross the groove's axis, and there the steepest tilt flips and points back left. I slalom down the gorge in a slow zigzag, crossing the trough again and again, never able to ride the axis straight, because straight along the axis is not the steepest tilt at any single step.

而这一切的代价开始啃噬我。每走一步信就更沉，每过一天雾就更冷，我惊惧地意识到：我永远无法判断真正的谷底是就在前面，还是在下一道壁外的整整第二道山脉之后。因为我只看得见局部的下倾，我无法测出自己距目标还有多远。我此生最长的一程，也许三个弯道后便止于一个我已曾一次误当作谷底的凹地，也许它根本不会结束。

And the cost of all this begins to gnaw at me. Each step the letter is heavier, each day the mist is colder, and I realize with dread that I can never tell whether the true floor lies just ahead or a whole second range beyond the next wall. Because I see only the local tilt, I cannot measure my distance from the goal. The longest journey of my life might end three bends from now in a hollow I have already mistaken once for the floor, or it might never end at all.

那惊惧在第四十三天清晨落成了事实。脚下的地平了，然后朝我能触到的每一侧都微微向上弯去——一只浅浅的洼地，半嵌在山腰，远高于河流所等候的真正谷底。此刻最陡的下倾向着洼地自己的中心指成一圈。我的腿带我绕着这只小凹坑的边沿转了一圈又一圈，每一圈只低一毫，旋即又更陡地指向中心。我被困在半山腰的一汪水洼里，而那禁我升高的诅咒，也同样禁我离去。

That dread hardens into fact on the forty-third morning. The ground beneath me flattens, then curves gently upward on every side I can feel—a shallow basin cupped in the mountainside, high above the true floor where the river waits. The steepest tilt now points in a circle, back toward the basin's own center. My legs carry me round and round the rim of this little hollow, each lap lower by a hair, then steeper toward the center again. I am trapped in a puddle on the hillside, and the curse that forbids me to rise also forbids me to leave.

在我下方，远远的下方，我能听见那条真正的河，那真正的谷底，在它一直流淌的地方流着。我知道它就在那里。但它与我之间，隆起一道我永不能攀的岩壁。我迈出下一步，按诅咒唯一容许的方式——向下，朝这只浅洼的中心——而我心里清楚，正如我四十三天来每个清晨都清楚的那样：我将在这只小洼地里永远地走下去，一毫一毫地降，绕着它的中心打转，而真正的谷底就在一道我生来无法跨越的墙下，等着。

Below me, far below, I can hear the true river, the real floor, running where it has always run. I know it is there. But between it and me rises a wall of rock I may never climb. I take my next step the only way the curse allows—downward, toward the center of this shallow hollow—and I know, as I have known for forty-three mornings, that I will walk this little basin forever, descending by hairs, circling its center, while the true floor waits below a wall I am not made to cross.

注解：这是梯度下降（gradient descent）：在当前点测出函数值上升最快的方向，沿其反方向走一小步使函数值下降，只看脚下局部坡度、无前瞻、单调下降，故可能困在半山腰的局部极小（local minimum）或鞍点而非全局最小，在狭长弯曲的峡谷中呈之字形反复越过谷轴缓慢下行。

注解：This is gradient descent（梯度下降）: at the current point, measure the direction in which the function rises fastest, then take a small step opposite it so that the value decreases—looking only at the local slope underfoot, with no foresight, descending monotonically—so one may get stuck in a local minimum（局部极小）or a saddle point（鞍点）on the hillside rather than reaching the global minimum, and in a long winding gorge one advances in a slow zigzag, crossing the trough axis again and again.
